Know Us

An honourable institution, Sabeelul Hidaya Islamic College, is run by the CH Kunjeen Musliyar Smaraka Trust. As of right now, it is affiliated with Darul Huda Islamic University, which has more than 30 affiliated institutions. Sabeelul Hidaya Islamic College is the most prestigious among them. In every way, Sabeelul Hidaya comes first, either ahead of or after Darul Huda.
